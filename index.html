About ITE

Current Students

The Institute of Technical Education(ITE) was established on 1 April 1992 as a post-secondary institution. An education from ITE provides graduates with good job and career advancement opportunities, as well as opportunities to go for further education and training.

Adopting a 'One ITE system, Three Colleges' Education Model, ITE is ready to provide exciting, high-quality, relevant and market-responsive learning for the global economy. ITE offers a wide range of courses to school leavers and working adults, with courses from the School of Applied & Health Sciences, Business & Services, Design & Media, Electronics and Info-Comm Technology, Engineering and Hospitality.
